About

THE SMART LOCALS (Pty) Ltd is a level 1 BEEE Contributor with 100% Black Ownership, having been established by Sihle Yotsi year 2021. The company was developed to provide clients with easy access to multple services within a local community.

With Covid-19 pendamic, we have realized it is difficult for our people to interact as per normal and going to public spaces for personal needs. Our mission is to provice a smart platform to our community that brings most of their personal needs and duties at the comfort of their homes. By doing so we are also keeping our community safe and clean from the Covid-19 pandemic.
